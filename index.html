AI Ethical Literacy

Welcome to AI Ethical Literacy

We are a student-led organization dedicated to building critical artificial intelligence skills. We hope to cultivate a solid foundation for the responsible and ethical use of AI and foster a future that uses these tools for good!

Who We Are

Founder and president Reese Fojas was a high school Junior when first creating this organization, in hopes to fulfill her Girl Scout Gold Award. With a passion in computer science and a strong interest in artificial intelligence, Reese saw an issue within her peers from the wrongful use of AI tools. She created a series of workshops for her community to teach students about AI to create an environment where anyone can use AI in a positive way to benefit society.

Our Mission

Our mission is to educate and instill foundational skills such as communication and language through informational workshops that teach prompt engineering, discuss ethical considerations, and provide tools to learn with AI. By promoting artificial intelligence literacy, we can create a future where we can responsibly and ethically use AI for good as it is rapidly being implemented in our everyday life.

Our Educational Workshops

We build workshops tailored mostly towards student age brackets, but open to curious minds of all generations.

🚀 AI Basics

Target Age: All ages

The fundamentals of artificial intelligence. Students explored AI in everyday life, how to detect AI-generated content, and ways it can shape their learning and the world around them.

🎨 Using AI to the Fullest Extent

Target Age: Grades 3+

In this workshop, students learned the definition of artificial intelligence, its ethical considerations, and how to prompt AI in an educational and beneficial way.

🧬 AI Mastery

Target Age: High schoolers and adults

An advanced lesson on how AI works, how to detect AI in the media, and the best way to ethically prompt it.
